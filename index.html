1 General Refund Policy

All sales of Robux packages are final by default. Because we deliver digital goods (Robux) and process payments via cryptocurrency — which are irreversible on the blockchain — we cannot offer automatic refunds.

However, we are committed to resolving genuine issues. If a problem occurs on our end, we will make it right.

2 When You Are Eligible for a Refund

You may request a refund or redelivery in the following situations:

Non-delivery — your order was paid and confirmed but Robux were not delivered within 24 hours.
Incorrect delivery — you received fewer Robux than what you paid for, due to our error.
Technical failure — a platform error on our side caused your order to fail after payment was confirmed.
Duplicate charge — you were charged twice for the same order due to a system error.

In eligible cases, we will issue a redelivery or store credit as the primary resolution. Cryptocurrency refunds will only be issued if redelivery is not possible, and are subject to a processing review of up to 5 business days.

3 When You Are NOT Eligible for a Refund

Order was successfully delivered to your Roblox account.
You provided an incorrect Roblox username at checkout.
You changed your mind after a completed and delivered order.
Your Roblox account was banned by Roblox after delivery (not our responsibility).
Refund requested more than 72 hours after the order was placed.
Account was suspended for abuse of our platform.
A Creator Task was activated — once a task is activated, the payment is non-refundable regardless of usage.
Credits are in pending or active status — credits become non-refundable once added to your account.
